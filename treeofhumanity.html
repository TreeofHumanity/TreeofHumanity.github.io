The Question

We measure what we value.
But do we value what we measure?

Civilisation has built extraordinary systems to measure and grow economic value. GDP. Stock markets. Corporate profits. But there is no equivalent system for measuring positive human contribution.

What we measure
  • GDP & economic growth
  • Stock market performance
  • Corporate profits
  • Financial returns
What we don't measure
  • Environmental restoration
  • Community care & support
  • Cultural creation
  • Human contribution

The Model

Built on Dana —
voluntary giving

Dana is the ancient Vedic principle of unconditional contribution. It is not just a philosophy here. It is the business model.

Artists donate work. Buyers pay what feels right. Every sale splits transparently — and every transaction plants a real tree.
